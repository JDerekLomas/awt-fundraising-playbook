## Best Practices for Contact

1. **Research First:** Review foundation websites and guidelines before reaching out
2. **Be Specific:** Contact the appropriate program officer or use designated email addresses
3. **Timeline:** Allow 3-5 business days for responses (some foundations specify longer)
4. **Portal Registration:** Register for grant portals well in advance of deadlines
5. **Follow Guidelines:** Some foundations prefer specific contact methods (online forms vs. email)
6. **Geographic Restrictions:** Note which foundations fund internationally vs. US/UK only
7. **Invitation-Only:** Some foundations (Mellon, Knight in 2025) operate by invitation
