The Authenticity Playbook

Research shows authentic content outperforms polished corporate messaging by 29%. Here's how to resonate:

1

Real Over Polished

Smartphone footage beats Hollywood production. Show real progress, real challenges, real moments. Gen Z donors are 41% more likely to give when content feels authentic.

2

Stories Over Stats

Lead with individual stories, then add numbers. "This 1497 book by Ficino..." resonates more than "5,000 books need digitizing."

3

Vulnerability Builds Trust

Share challenges alongside wins. "We hit a roadblock..." gets more engagement than constant celebration.

4

Engage, Don't Broadcast

Organizations that prioritize commenting see 41% boost in reach. Reply to everyone. Start conversations.
