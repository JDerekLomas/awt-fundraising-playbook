Quick Outreach Scripts

LinkedIn/Email intro for AI company decision makers:

"Hi [Name], Quick question: What if we could train AI on the best of human thought, not just what happened to make it online? I'm with the Ancient Wisdom Trust, and we're doing something that might interest you: using AI to translate 5,000 rare Renaissance Latin texts—works by Ficino, Bruno, Agrippa, and other thinkers who shaped the Western intellectual tradition. 95% of Renaissance books lack translations. The AI systems being built today are learning from a radically incomplete picture of human knowledge. We're working to fix that. And we think the tech community should be part of this effort. Would love to connect if this resonates."

For publishing company outreach:

"Hi [Name], I wanted to introduce you to an opportunity that could yield a remarkable pipeline of never-before-published content. The Ancient Wisdom Trust is translating 5,000 Renaissance texts that have never been available in English—works by Marsilio Ficino, Cornelius Agrippa, Paracelsus, and other foundational thinkers. We're offering publishing partners early access to translation rights and the chance to be part of bringing these lost classics to modern readers. Would you be interested in learning more about a potential partnership?"

For luxury brand outreach:

"Dear [Name], I'm reaching out about an exclusive cultural partnership opportunity. The Ancient Wisdom Trust preserves the UNESCO-recognized Bibliotheca Philosophica Hermetica in Amsterdam—one of the world's most significant collections of rare Renaissance manuscripts. We're seeking partners who appreciate the highest form of cultural patronage. Benefits include private events at the Embassy of the Free Mind, exclusive access to newly translated works, and association with a preservation effort of lasting significance. I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss how a partnership might serve [Brand]'s cultural mission."
