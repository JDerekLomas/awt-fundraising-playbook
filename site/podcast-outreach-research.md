## GENERAL OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Finding Contact Information (Step-by-Step)

1. **Website Investigation**
   - Go to podcast website
   - Check: "Contact," "About," "Guest," "Booking," "Work With Us" pages
   - Look in footer for email addresses
   - Check "Press" or "Media" sections

2. **Social Media**
   - Instagram DMs (often most responsive)
   - Twitter DMs or public @mentions
   - LinkedIn messages (for professional approach)
   - YouTube "About" section

3. **Podcast Platforms**
   - Apple Podcasts, Spotify show notes
   - Often include website links or contact info
   - Check episode descriptions

4. **Email Pattern Guessing**
   - Common formats: contact@, booking@, info@, podcast@, [firstname]@
   - Use tools like Hunter.io or RocketReach to verify
   - Try multiple variations

5. **Patreon/Membership Platforms**
   - Many hosts more accessible to supporters
   - Join at basic tier for direct access
   - Often have exclusive contact methods

6. **Network/Agent Search**
   - Some hosts have booking agents
   - Check if part of larger podcast network
   - Network contact forms may route to host

### Best Practices

1. **Personalize Every Pitch**
   - Reference specific episodes
   - Explain why THIS audience cares
   - Show you understand their show

2. **Lead with Story, Not Fundraising**
   - Position as fascinating content first
   - Mention donor potential secondarily
   - Let natural interest drive support

3. **Provide Easy Yes**
   - Offer pre-interview call
   - Send background materials
   - Be flexible on format/timing
   - Prepare talking points

4. **Follow Up Strategically**
   - Wait 1 week, then follow up once
   - Try different contact method
   - Don't spam multiple channels simultaneously

5. **Build Relationships**
   - Engage with their content first
   - Comment thoughtfully on social media
   - Share their episodes
   - Become known before pitching
