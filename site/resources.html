Pitch Elements

One-Liner

"We're using AI to unlock 500 years of hidden wisdom."

Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

"The Ancient Wisdom Trust is digitizing and translating 5,000 rare Renaissance texts that shaped Newton, Copernicus, and the foundations of Western thought. 95% of these books have never been translated. We're using AI to change that, making this wisdom freely accessible to scholars and training AI on humanity's best thinking, not just what happened to make it online."

The Three Stats

  • 95% of Renaissance books lack translations
  • 70% of books from 1450-1700 remain undigitized
  • 5,000 texts we're working to unlock

Objection Handlers

"Why is this urgent?" These books are physically deteriorating. Every year we wait, pages crumble. And AI systems are being trained right now on incomplete human knowledge.
"Why AI translation?" Traditional scholarly translation is too slow and expensive. AI lets us work at scale while maintaining quality through expert review.
"Why should AI companies care?" Training data matters. AI systems trained on Reddit threads miss the depth of human thought. These texts contain the foundations of philosophy, science, and ethics.
"How will you ensure quality?" AI translation is reviewed by classical scholars. We're not replacing human expertise, we're scaling it.
