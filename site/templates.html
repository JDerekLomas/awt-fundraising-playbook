I'd welcome the opportunity to share more about this work with you. Would you have 20 minutes for a call in the coming weeks? Warm regards, [Your Name] [Title] Ancient Wisdom Trust P.S. I'd be happy to send you a translated excerpt from one of our texts—perhaps a never-before-translated passage from Marsilio Ficino or Cornelius Agrippa. Just say the word.

Email Best Practices

Subject Lines

  • Keep under 50 characters
  • Create curiosity or urgency
  • Personalize when possible
  • A/B test variations

Timing

  • Major donors: Personal, not mass emails
  • Follow up after 7-10 days
  • Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
  • Best times: 10am or 2pm
