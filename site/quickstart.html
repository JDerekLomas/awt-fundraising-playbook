Quick Start Tonight

10 high-priority outreach targets you can contact RIGHT NOW

You've got this.

Fundraising is just telling people about something amazing and giving them a chance to be part of it. These people are interested in exactly what you're doing. Start with one message. Then another.

Step 1: Copy Your Twitter/X DM Script

Use this for tech founders and influencers. Keep it short - you can elaborate if they respond.

Hi [Name], I'm working on something you might find interesting - we're using AI to translate 5,000 rare Renaissance Latin texts that have never been available in English. Think Ficino, Agrippa, Bruno - the source material of Western esoteric thought. 95% of Renaissance books have never been translated. We're changing that. Would love to tell you more if you're curious. No pressure either way. [Your name] ancientwisdomtrust.org

Step 3: After You Send Tonight's Messages

Remember

  • Most people don't respond. That's normal. It only takes one yes.
  • You're not asking for a favor. You're offering them a chance to be part of something meaningful.
  • Personalization matters. Reference their specific interests when possible.
  • Follow up. The second message often gets the response.
  • Celebrate small wins. Every sent message is progress.
